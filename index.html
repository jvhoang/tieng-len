Rules — Tiến Lên

Core (Pagat + Wikipedia canonical)

  • 52-card deck. 13 cards each (2–4 players).
  • Rank: 2 > A > K > Q > J > 10 > … > 3. Suits: ♥ > ♦ > ♣ > ♠
  • Combos: single, pair, triple, quad, sequence (≥3 consecutive), double-sequence (≥3 consecutive pairs).
  • First lead must include 3♠.
  • Play higher same-type combo or pass. Pass = locked out of current trick.
  • Bombs beat 2s: any quad, 3+ pair seq vs single 2; 4+ pairs vs two 2s; 5+ pairs vs three 2s. Playable after pass.
  • First to zero cards wins the round. Last player holding cards loses.
